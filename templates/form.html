Sock Pattern Generator

A knitting pattern generator for socks to fit feet of any size! These socks are knit toe-up with a short-row toe and heel.

This is a very basic recipe for making a sock, and assumes you are already familiar with sock-making in general and with techniques such as provisional cast on and short rows. Choose your preferred method or take a look at the useful links page.

No particular stitch pattern is suggested for the sock instep or leg. Choose your own after you finish the toe. A simple ribbing pattern always works well.

Warning! If you are using a stitch pattern, make sure your pattern repeat divides evenly into your total stitches. Your total stitches will be twice the number of stitches you cast on for the toe.
